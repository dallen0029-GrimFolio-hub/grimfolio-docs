Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about GrimFolio — what it is, how it works, and who it's for.

What is GrimFolio?
GrimFolio is a creative project planning tool built for people who make immersive experiences — haunted houses, escape rooms, theater productions, film, TTRPGs, live events, and anything else with a lot of moving parts. It gives you a structured way to capture ideas, map how everything connects, and manage your production from concept to opening night.
What kinds of projects can I use it for?
Any creative project with interconnected pieces. A 30-room haunted attraction and a 5-puzzle escape room are both great fits. So is a short film, a tabletop campaign, a cosplay build, or a community theater production. GrimFolio works at any scale — you don't need a massive project to get value from it.
Why use GrimFolio instead of Notion, Trello, or a spreadsheet?
Those tools are built for generic task management. GrimFolio is built specifically for creative productions — the three-stage system mirrors how creative projects actually develop, the canvas lets you map spatial and narrative relationships between elements, and Grim understands your project deeply enough to give you real input instead of generic advice. It's the difference between a tool that tolerates your workflow and one built around it.
How is GrimFolio different from a mind map tool?
Mind maps capture structure but don't hold production details. In GrimFolio, every card carries notes, checklists, budget lines, photos, inventory assignments, and more. Your map is also your production bible — not just a diagram.
How does the three-stage system work?
Stage 1 (The Pile) is pure capture — dump every idea as a card without worrying about structure. Stage 2 (Build) is where you drag cards onto a canvas, connect them, and map how everything fits together. Stage 3 (Production) replaces the canvas with a customizable command center — checklists, budgets, schedules, crew, and your walk-through all in one place. You move between stages at your own pace and can always go back.
Is it actually free?
Yes. The Free plan is genuinely free — no credit card required. You get 3 projects, document exports, and a preview of Grim. If you outgrow it, paid plans start at a one-time purchase with no subscription required.
What do I get on the free plan?
3 projects, 50 inspiration images, all document exports (checklist, budget, card summary, walk-through guide), and a limited preview of Grim. No credit card needed to sign up.
Why does it cost money?
Building and running GrimFolio — the AI infrastructure, storage, worker compute, and ongoing development — has real costs. Paid plans fund continued development and keep the free tier genuinely useful. We'd rather charge fairly than show you ads or sell your data.
Is the AI optional?
Completely. Grim is there when you want her and out of the way when you don't. The Base Unlimited plan has no Grim access at all and is still a full-featured planning tool. Every core feature works without AI.
What can Grim actually do?
Grim reads your entire project — cards, connections, notes, budgets, linked documents, crew schedule, and linked projects — and uses that context to give you specific, useful input. She can create cards, build checklists, suggest connections, generate project summaries, flag gaps in your plan, and help you think through problems. She's not a generic chatbot. She knows your project.
Is my data used to train AI?
No. Your project data is never used to train AI models. Grim uses your content as context for your session only. We do not share or sell your data.
Is it safe to connect my Google account?
Yes. Google sign-in uses the standard OAuth flow — we never see your Google password. If you connect Google Drive, GrimFolio only has access to files it creates. We cannot read the rest of your Drive. You can disconnect Google at any time from Account Settings, and you can revoke access entirely from your Google account security settings.
Do I need a Google account to use GrimFolio?
No. You can sign up with any email address. Google sign-in is optional and only required if you want to use Google Drive backup features.
Can I use GrimFolio on mobile?
Yes. GrimFolio works on mobile browsers. The canvas is optimized for desktop, but Stage 1, Stage 3, and most tools are fully usable on a phone or tablet.
Can I collaborate with others?
Studio plan users can invite up to 5 collaborators per project. Collaborators share the canvas, card details, comments, Team Notepad, and Walk-Through. The Activity Log tracks every change with attribution. You can assign cards to team members and @mention them in comments — they'll get a notification in the app.
Can I assign cards to team members?
Yes. Open a card's detail drawer and click the Assignees field to pick from the project owner plus any accepted collaborators. Each assigned person gets a colored initials flag on the card tile on the canvas. Assignee data can be exported as an Ownership Report — a PDF that groups all cards by team member.
Can I add tags, priority, or due dates to cards?
Yes — all three are available on every card. Open the card detail drawer and set them in the right panel. Tags let you label cards with freeform keywords for filtering and searching. Priority marks urgency (None, Low, Medium, High, Critical). Due dates show as a pill on the card's canvas tile and appear in calendar and list views.
Is there a list view or a board view?
Yes. In Stage 2 you can switch from the canvas to a sortable List View (a table of all your cards with title, type, status, priority, due date, tags, and assignees) or a Board View (a Kanban-style layout grouped by status). Both are accessible from the toolbar in Stage 2. The canvas is still the primary view — List and Board are alternate lenses on the same cards.
Can I see all cards across all my projects in one place?
Yes. The All Projects Board on the Dashboard gives you a cross-project Kanban showing every card from every project grouped by status. Use the project tabs across the top to filter down to one project or keep the All view for the full picture.
Can checklist items repeat automatically?
Yes. Individual checklist items inside a card can be set to recur on a schedule — daily, weekly, monthly, or a custom pattern. When the date arrives the item resets to unchecked automatically. This is separate from calendar events, which do not repeat. Recurring checklist items are configured inside the card detail drawer.
What are Card Templates?
Card Templates let you save a card's checklist, budget lines, and notes as a reusable starting point. Apply a template to any card to instantly populate it with that content instead of building from scratch every time. Templates are stored per project and managed in Stage 2.
What is the Ownership Report?
The Ownership Report is a PDF export that groups every card in the project by who is assigned to it, with unassigned cards at the end. It includes a summary bar showing total cards, assigned count, team member count, and unassigned count. Access it from the Export button dropdown. It's available to any plan but requires at least one card to have an assignee to be useful.
How do notifications work?
GrimFolio sends in-app notifications when a card is assigned to you, you are @mentioned in a comment, or you are removed from a card's assignees. The bell icon in the top bar shows a badge when there are unread notifications. Click it to open the panel and see the 50 most recent. Click Mark all read to clear the badge.
What happens to my projects if I cancel my plan?
Your projects stay. You drop to Free plan limits — 3 projects become accessible, the rest are preserved but not visible until you upgrade again. You never lose data.
Can I export my data? Am I locked in?
No lock-in. Export any project as a Quick Export (JSON) or Full Export (ZIP) at any time, on any plan. Full Export includes every card, connection, image, document, and your Grim chat history. Import it back into any GrimFolio account or keep it as an archive.
Can I switch plans?
Yes, any time. Upgrade instantly. Downgrade at the end of your billing period.
What is a Founder membership?
The first 100 paid members get 20% off their plan forever, a gold G avatar, and a Founder Badge on their profile. Use code FOUNDER at checkout. Once 100 spots are gone, they're gone.
What does GrimFolio NOT do?
GrimFolio is not a ticket sales platform, a point-of-sale system, a staff payroll tool, or a full enterprise project management suite. It's focused on the creative and production planning side of immersive experiences. If you need those other tools, GrimFolio works alongside them — not instead of them.
